Venue Details

The Edgbaston Campus is the central campus of the University of Birmingham, and is where the majority of academic and administrative departments are located. The clock tower at the centre of the campus is known as Old Joe, after Joseph Chamberlain. Old Joe is considered to be an emblem of the University.
